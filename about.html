Treats Without Tricks is a web-app developed during Kent Hack Enough 2018 that aims to make trick-or-treating easier for parents and children alike. Inspiration was taken from the Teal Pumpkin Project, an initiative to make Halloween more inclusive for those with allergies. The app allows users to input addresses and input information for each house, such as or not a house is open for trick-or-treaters, whether or not itâ€™s safe for those with allergies, and other parameters.
